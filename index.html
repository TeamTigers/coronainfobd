Case study

  • 8th march,2020

    First time 3 patients. 2 were Italy-returnees. Third one was the wife one of them (They returned home after their treatment)

  • 14th march,2020

    Health Minister Zahid Maleque said 2 more corona virus cases were detected (Italy-1, Germany-1)

  • 16th march,2020

    3 more detected positive of COVID-19 from the same family. (1 Female, 2 Child)

  • 17th march,2020

    2 more from that same family whom were detected on 16th march,2020 (Italy-1, 1 affected by USA Expatriate. That Expatriate already went back to USA)

  • 18th march,2020

    4 new person detected corona positive (2 returnees of Italy(1 male), 1 from Kuwait, another one is affected by an old corona patient) (Total: 3 man, 1 woman)

  • 19th march,2020

    4 person detected corona positive. 2 of them are men age of 65 years and 32 years. 1 woman of age 22 years. They were affected by their relative who came from Italy. Last one was 30 years old male who was in the Isolation procedure in Chuadanga Sadar Hospital and came from Italy.

The Institute of Epidemiology, Disease Control and Research (IEDCR) increased the hotline numbers to report any suspected cases of coronavirus to 12 from four, after three cases were confirmed in Bangladesh on Sunday. Sources at the IEDCR also said that, if contacted and according to necessity, their special medical teams would visit suspected coronavirus patients' homes and collect samples for testing. According to the IEDCR, the hotline numbers were increased as the coronavirus alert level has been raised to three from one.
